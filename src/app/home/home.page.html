My Story

Hi there! I would like to introduce myself to you. My name is Valentyna, I came to Canada from Ukraine, Kyiv in the spring of 2022 and got very lucky to end up here, in beautiful Niagara-on-the-Lake.

I started to make and sell Ukrainian traditional VARENYKY... We call them VARENYKY because in Ukrainian "TO BOIL" translates as "VARYTY" (варити).

Back there, in my grandma's kitchen, we often got together and made them from scratch. Children, grandchildren -- for our family, we needed to make 100-150 pieces per night, boil them, add fried onion with bacon, and sour cream on top.

Now, when I and many other Ukrainians do not have the opportunity to gather and cook for their families at home, I started to do it here, in Canada. For my friends: Canadians, Ukrainians, Irish, Polish, Indian, Scottish, French, German, Italian, Chinese, English, Dutch, Portuguese, and others.

The most important thing is that I want to share our culture and show a piece of Ukraine to the world through food. It is very important to talk about my country, so I decided to do it this way.
