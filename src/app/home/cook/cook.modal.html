How to cook...

Salty Varenyky

  1. Get a pot with water. There should be enough water to put all the pierogi underwater.
  2. Boil the water.
  3. Put pierogi in and wait for them to float (stir them sometimes so they don't stick to the pot).
  4. When the pierogi start floating, wait for another 2 minutes.
  5. Put them in a large bowl, add some butter/ sour cream / fried onion & bacon, and mix it up!
